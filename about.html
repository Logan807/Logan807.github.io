Logan Riddell


I am a web and graphic designer in New Hampshire, currently enrolled in F.I.R.S.T. Institute's Graphic Design and Web Development program to grow my skills. I bring a unique and flexible design style, and value style, user experience, and usability based on my experiences with video games, where such considerations are imperative and often a good indicator for overall quality. I enjoy working in these programs, and work hard to make things others will enjoy.
